What You Should Understand Before Linking a Deposit Account

Linking a deposit account help keep the Scriptbill Network secure to help trading to and fro your fiat account automatic and smooth

Linking a Deposit account syncs your Scriptbill account with the account linked. This also means syncing your Scriptbill transactions with your deposit account transaction. For instance, withdrawing cash from your Scriptbill account using your deposit account debit card on a POS transaction.

Unexpected transactions may be recorded from your deposit account providers. Many of these transactions are virtually emitted automatically by Scriptbill to help secure the network. They don't reduce your credibility, except they are real transactions emitted by you or anyone with unauthorized access to your deposit account

It's advisable you manage account you link with your Scriptbill account with your deposit account service provider apps or sofwares integrated with Scriptbills. This way your deposit account syncing with Scriptbills is more smoother, healthier and more secure, as it becomes impossible for a third party to access your deposit account.

Deposit Account not properly synced with Scriptbills would not work. Without deposit account your liquidity with Scriptbills will not work. So ensure you properly sync at least a deposit account with Scriptbank using Scriptbills Cryptonote System to ensure liquidity from Unlimited Loans and Investment opportunities with Scriptbank
